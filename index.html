Welcome to My Risk Assessment!

The purpose of this web application is to help leaders:

  1. Identify the risks their agencies face
  2. Note gaps in existing risk management strategies
  3. Obtain specific advice to strengthen risk identification and risk management

After completing this assessment you will have a deeper awareness of the myriad risks facing your organization. As you answer questions about your mission, scope of services and operations, a profile of your organization will be created within the application. That profile will enable you to generate a report containing targeted risk advice from the experts at the Nonprofit Risk Management Center. Your final report will present feedback on your exposures, as well as specific advice to share with the leadership team at your organization.

If you get “stuck” or experience technical difficulty, don’t hestiate to contact the Nonprofit Risk Management Center for help. We can be reached via email 24/7: info@nonprofitrisk.org or during business hours at 703.777.3504. Technical assistance is available free to My Risk Assessment subscribers, as well as Affiliate Members of the Center. To sign up as an Affiliate Member, visit www.nonprofitrisk.org/affiliates.

Let's Get Started!

Now that you have logged in, it’s time to begin the Risk Assessment for your organization. Here are the steps:

  • Click the “Assessment” tab at the top of the screen.
  • Work through the modules by answering the sequential questions.
  • As you complete a module you will be prompted to continue with the next module, or you can review your answers or view a draft report with feedback based on completed modules.
  • Take a break any time, and log back in when it’s convenient. All of your answers will be saved and you’ll be able to “pick up where you left off” when you return.

If you get “stuck” or experience technical difficulty, don’t hestiate to contact the Nonprofit Risk Management Center for help. We can be reached via email 24/7: info@nonprofitrisk.org or during business hours at 703-777-3504.
